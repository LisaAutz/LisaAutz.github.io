ME

Recent work: Everyone In

Launched in early 2018, Everyone In is a campaign to end homelessness in L.A. County through supportive housing and wraparound medical, mental health, and social services. Through compelling content, digital organizing and activist training, and elevating the stories of advocates and Angelenos with lived experience, Everyone In has become a force to energize communities about homelessness solutions and hold elected officials accountable.

See more: www.everyoneinla.org

Recent work: I AM 2018

In light of the Janus decision and the 50th anniversary of Martin Luther King’s assassination, AFSCME, the Church of God in Christ, and dozens of other labor organizations and nonprofits came together in Memphis for a three day protest and reaffirmation of Martin Luther King’s legacy: equality and justice for all people. Helping lead the digital, outreach, and organizing campaign, we helped build a powerful show of unity and strength—featuring Senator Bernie Sanders, Reverend Al Sharpton, Jesse Jackson, and Common.
